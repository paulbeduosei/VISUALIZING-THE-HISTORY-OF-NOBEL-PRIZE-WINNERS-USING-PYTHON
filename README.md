This project analyzes the complete dataset of Nobel Prize winners from 1901 to 2023, covering all categories including Physics, Chemistry, Medicine, Literature, Economics, and Peace.

Using data from the official Nobel Prize API, it explores trends, patterns, and insights about laureates, such as award distribution over time, country representation, and demographic highlights. 

The goal is to provide an accessible overview of Nobel Prize history and encourage further exploration of interesting questions within the dataset.
